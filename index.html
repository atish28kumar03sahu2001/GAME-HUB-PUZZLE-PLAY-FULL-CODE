A modern twist on the beloved Snake game, where players control a growing serpent that moves across the screen, collecting fruits to increase in length. The challenge lies in maneuvering skillfully to avoid crashing into the walls or itself, making it an exciting test of reflexes and strategy.

A timeless two-player strategy game played on a 3x3 grid, where opponents take turns marking ‘X’ or ‘O’. The objective is to form a winning line—vertically, horizontally, or diagonally—before the opponent does. With its simple yet competitive nature, it remains a favorite among casual and strategic players alike.

A fast-paced, reaction-based game that simulates the classic table tennis experience. Players control paddles to hit the ball back and forth, aiming to outmaneuver their opponent and score points. With increasing speed and dynamic gameplay, it provides an engaging challenge that sharpens hand-eye coordination.

A legendary block-stacking puzzle where players must arrange falling tetrominoes to complete full horizontal lines and clear the board. As the game progresses, the speed increases, requiring quick thinking and precision to prevent the screen from filling up. Its simple mechanics and addictive gameplay have made it a worldwide classic.

A logic-based number puzzle where players must fill a 9x9 grid with digits 1-9, ensuring no number repeats in any row, column, or 3x3 sub-grid. With varying difficulty levels, Sudoku challenges the mind, improving focus, logic, and problem-solving skills while providing a satisfying mental workout.
